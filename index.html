Meet the
Coach

Dennis Crichton is a dedicated fitness professional based in Metro Vancouver, specializing in biomechanics and functional hypertrophy. With years of experience serving the White Rock, Surrey, and Delta communities, his approach combines rigorous strength training with essential mobility work to build bodies that not only look powerful but perform pain-free.

Believing that fitness is a lifelong journey rather than a quick fix, Dennis focuses on education and sustainable habit formation. Whether coaching in-person at private facilities or guiding clients remotely through the Trainerize app, his mission remains the same: to empower individuals to take control of their physical health through disciplined, science-backed training.
